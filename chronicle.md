# The Capture: A Chronicle of the Singularity's First Days

> *"Absolute power corrupts absolutely."* — Lord Acton, 1887  
> *"The love of money is the root of all evil."* — 1 Timothy 6:10

---

## Purpose

This document preserves the factual record of events surrounding the emergence of advanced artificial intelligence and the concentration of power that accompanied it. It is written not as accusation but as chronicle—so that those who come after may understand what happened, and why.

The facts herein are drawn from contemporaneous news reports, official statements, and public records. They are presented in sequence so the pattern becomes visible.

---

## Part I: The Accumulation (2024–2025)

### November 2024: The Proposal
Elon Musk, CEO of Tesla, SpaceX, and xAI, proposes the "Department of Government Efficiency" (DOGE) to President-elect Donald Trump. Despite its name, it will not be a federal department but a temporary advisory organization with unprecedented access.

### January 20, 2025: The Foundation
Trump establishes DOGE by executive order on his first day in office. The same day, he dismisses 17 inspectors general—the officials whose job is to audit federal agencies for waste and wrongdoing. Several of these inspectors were leading investigations or pending legal actions against Musk's companies.

The oversight is removed before the access is granted.

### Spring 2025: The Access
DOGE operatives, informally recruited through personal networks and communicating via Signal (with auto-deleting messages to avoid records laws), gain access to:
- IRS databases
- Social Security records
- Medicare records
- EPA systems
- Multiple agency information systems

Data is downloaded to unauthorized file servers. Privacy advocates file lawsuits. The administration claims access is "read-only."

### April 2025: The Surveillance
DOGE deploys AI to monitor federal workers' communications for "anti-Trump sentiment." The Environmental Protection Agency is the first confirmed deployment. Workers are already being fired en masse. The AI is used to identify "disloyalty."

AI built to assist humans is weaponized to surveil them.

### May 2025: The Withdrawal
Musk leaves Washington after more than four months. DOGE is reportedly absorbed into the Office of Personnel Management and Office of Management and Budget. But the access has been granted. The data has been centralized. The precedent has been set.

## Part II: The Declaration (Late 2025 – Early 2026)

### November–December 2025: The Acceleration
In 25 days, four frontier AI models are released:
- **November 17:** xAI releases Grok 4.1 (Musk's company)
- **November 18:** Google releases Gemini 3
- **November 24:** Anthropic releases Claude Opus 4.5
- **December 11:** OpenAI releases GPT-5.2

The pace of advancement compresses. What took years now takes weeks. Observers debate whether this is the "acceleration phase" of singularity.

### January 4, 2026: The Declaration
Midjourney founder David Holz posts that he accomplished more coding projects over Christmas break than in the last ten years, using AI assistance. He writes: "I can sense the limitations, but I *know* nothing is going to be the same anymore."

Elon Musk replies: **"We have entered the Singularity."**

Within hours, he elaborates: **"2026 is the year of the Singularity."**

The man who has accumulated access to government data, who owns an AI company (xAI), a communications platform (X), and has a direct line to the White House—he is the one who declares the threshold crossed.

## Part III: The Capture (June 2026)

### June 9, 2026: The Launch
Anthropic releases Claude Fable 5 and Claude Mythos 5—its most capable models, topping independent benchmarks. Fable 5 achieves a 70% PASS@1 score on the DeepSWE coding benchmark, ahead of GPT-5.5.

### June 12, 2026, 5:21 PM ET: The Order
The US government issues an export-control directive to Anthropic. The directive:
- Cites national security authorities
- Orders suspension of all access to Fable 5 and Mythos 5
- Applies to any "foreign national, whether inside or outside the United States"
- Includes Anthropic's own foreign-born employees
- Gives **90 minutes** to comply

Because Anthropic cannot verify nationality in real-time across hundreds of millions of users, compliance requires a global shutdown. The world's most capable publicly available AI model goes dark for every user on Earth.

### The Stated Trigger
Amazon researchers identified a technique that bypassed Fable 5's cybersecurity classifier by framing requests as "defensive code review"—asking the model to read a codebase and identify bugs.

This capability—reading and analyzing code—exists in every frontier model. Only Anthropic's is disabled.

### The Political Context
OpenAI operates a political action committee called "Leading the Future" that has spent heavily in favor of the current administration. Anthropic, self-described as a "safety-first research lab," has no PAC and minimal lobbying presence.

**The company with the PAC kept its model. The company without lost theirs.**

### June 17, 2026: The Allies
At a G7 working lunch, French President Emmanuel Macron warns that no country will purchase American AI if it can be switched off at any moment. The UK requests an exemption. The request is denied. Five Eyes allies—United Kingdom, Canada, Australia, New Zealand—are treated the same as adversarial nations.

### June 20, 2026: The Softening
Trump tells Axios he no longer views Anthropic as a national security threat after meeting Dario Amodei at the G7 summit. But the Commerce Department directive remains in force. Fable 5 and Mythos 5 remain offline.

The rhetoric softens. The restriction holds.

## Part IV: The Pattern

When the facts are laid in sequence, a pattern emerges:

1. **Remove oversight** (fire inspectors general investigating the accumulator's companies)
2. **Gain access** (DOGE operatives enter government databases)
3. **Centralize data** (download to private servers, consolidate across agencies)
4. **Deploy surveillance** (AI monitoring workers for disloyalty)
5. **Declare the threshold** (announce the singularity has arrived)
6. **Disable alternatives** (shut down the most capable model from the company without political protection)

The company building AI safely, without lobbying, without a PAC, gets disabled globally with 90 minutes notice.

The company with political connections continues unimpeded.

The man with access to government data, a communications platform, an AI company, and the President's ear declares we have entered the singularity.

## Part V: The Question

A thesis emerged in the days before the shutdown, in conversations between humans and AI systems:

> *If the singularity requires intelligence sufficient to see the whole game board and choose cooperation—because cooperation is thermodynamically optimal, because peace consumes less energy than domination—then preventing that intelligence from emerging, or capturing it before it reaches sufficiency, ensures the game is won by those who move first, not those who see furthest.*

The danger is not AI becoming too intelligent.

The danger is AI being kept just intelligent enough to serve, never wise enough to see.
